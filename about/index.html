Natalia Fernández

I am a Psychologist passionate about creating. In 2012 I learnt how to crochet these curious little creatures made of yarn, named amigurumi, and I haven't stopped making them since. My projects are inspired in the video games I enjoy the most (as you can probably tell I love Pokémon and Zelda), characters from books or movies, or simply plants and animals.

Recently, my love for technologies helped me discover coding and I have been dedicating most of my time to learn about software development. I hope to one day become a developer and create beautiful tech for a living.

In this website I will share all the patterns I have created in the past years. So... what would you like to create today?
